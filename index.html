About

AUREX is a digital scarcity project designed with a fixed supply, clean token logic, and a transparent long-term structure. The concept favors discipline over hype.

Scarcity by Design

Only 1000 AUX will ever exist. No inflation, no hidden minting, and no supply expansion.

Long-Term Structure

80% of the supply is locked through a dedicated timelock contract, reinforcing long-term alignment and trust.

Why AUREX

AUREX is not built as a high-noise token. It is positioned as a disciplined scarcity asset with transparent rules and a long-term narrative.

Simple Contract Logic

The token contract avoids unnecessary complexity. No taxes, no inflation, and no hidden mechanisms.

Verifiable On-Chain

Both the token and timelock contracts are publicly verified on Sepolia Etherscan, allowing anyone to inspect the source code.

Concentration Protection

A 2% anti-whale transfer limit is designed to reduce early concentration and extreme imbalance.

Built for Patience

AUREX is structured for long-term development rather than short-term price excitement.
